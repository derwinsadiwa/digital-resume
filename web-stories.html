Built a web stories

  • Live Demo

It's a web-based version of the popular Story format that allowing us to host and own our content, similar to stories on social media platforms

The goal is to reach more people and engage the audience by bringing stories to our website.

  • Built on Google AMP (Accelerated Mobile Pages) API, an open source framework created by Google developers
  • Can be hosted or linked to other websites directly
  • It helps generate traffic and increase brand visibility on the open web
  • Easy indexing and help our stories appear at the top of a Google search
  • Created different transition using AMP API
